About Me


My name is Mona Essaid and I am a full-stack developer from Los Angeles, CA. I am a recent graduate with a master’s degree in Software Development from the University of Glasgow in Scotland. Since my graduation, I have been working on personal projects, earning certifications, and fine-tuning my master’s thesis project with the goal of releasing it in early 2025. I graduated from the Cook Systems FastTrack program in December 2024. During the program, I completed backend projects in Java, SpringBoot, and Node, and frontend projects with html, CSS, JavaScript, and Angular. I look forward to continuing my career in software development.
